Short Bio

I am a Ph.D. student in the Program in Brain Research: Computation and Information Processing of the Edmond & Lily Safra Center for Brain Sciences. I am a member of the Computational Linguistics and Natural Language Processing lab at the School of Computer Science and Engineering of The Hebrew University of Jerusalem. My advisor is Prof. Ari Rappoport. I studied mathematics and computer science at the Open University of Israel, and completed my B.Sc. (cum laude) in 2010. Since 2008, I have been a software engineer at IBM Research.
